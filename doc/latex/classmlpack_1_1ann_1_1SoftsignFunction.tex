\subsection{Detailed Description}
The softsign function, defined by. 

\begin{eqnarray*} f(x) &=& \frac{x}{1 + |x|} \\ f'(x) &=& (1 - |x|)^2 \\ f(x) &=& \left\{ \begin{array}{lr} -\frac{y}{y-1} & : x > 0 \\ \frac{x}{1 + x} & : x \le 0 \end{array} \right. \end{eqnarray*}
