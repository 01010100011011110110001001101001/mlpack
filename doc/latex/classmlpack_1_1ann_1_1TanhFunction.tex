\subsection{Detailed Description}
The tanh function, defined by. 

\begin{eqnarray*} f(x) &=& \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} \\ f'(x) &=& 1 - \tanh^2(x) \\ f^{-1}(x) &=& \arctan(x) \end{eqnarray*}
