\subsection{Detailed Description}
The logistic function, defined by. 

\begin{eqnarray*} f(x) &=& \frac{1}{1 + e^{-x}} \\ f'(x) &=& f(x) * (1 - f(x)) \\ f^{-1}(y) &=& ln(\frac{y}{1-y}) \end{eqnarray*}
